Helpful Information
  1. To maintain healthy blood, it's important to consume a balanced diet rich in nutrients like iron, vitamins, and minerals.
  2. Regular exercise, such as walking or jogging, can improve blood circulation and overall health.
  3. To make the donation process more comfortable, be sure to eat a healthy meal and stay well-hydrated before your appointment.
  4. After donating blood, remember to drink plenty of fluids to stay hydrated and prevent dizziness.
  5. A great post-donation tip is to avoid heavy lifting or strenuous activities for at least 24 hours.
